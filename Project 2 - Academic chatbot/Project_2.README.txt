# Explanation of Scenario

I chose an academic research chatbot. It's purpose is to answer questions specifically regarding the efficacy of COVID-19 lockdowns. It uses a well-cited (more than 200 citations) paper. By adding additional papers, it's scope could be broadened beyond its limited data.
